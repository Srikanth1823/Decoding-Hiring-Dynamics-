# Decoding-Hiring-Dynamics-

### Executive Summary 
In the landscape of Hiring agencies, understanding the interplay between candidate attributes and the individual differences that set them apart is paramount. This project delves to answer a couple of research questions. 

### Problem
The project aimed to understand the factors influencing candidates' job offers and progression through the recruitment process, particularly their likelihood of receiving job offers at various stages. The study focused on a dataset containing 100,000 candidate records, tracking their progress through various recruitment stages.

### Data
The dataset consisted of candidate-specific information, including citizenship status, years of work experience, desired salary, primary skills, and time-stamped stage progression, listed job features, recruiter specific features etc.

### Analysis
We conducted a comprehensive analysis of the data, employing various statistical models. For the influence of candidate features on job offer analysis, I GLM mixed model with a binomial distribution and included random effects to account for individual variability. For stage wise progression, I employed survival analysis to estimate the hazard of progressing to the next stage
### Significance 
The business problem at hand revolves around the need to optimize the recruitment process by understanding the factors influencing a candidate’s chances of receiving a job offer and dynamics of progression through the various recruitment stages. So that the Hiring and staffing agencies better understand the market and successfully place its candidates. 

**Efficient Talent Acquisition**: Attracting and retaining top talent is crucial for business success, understanding how different candidate attributes affect their prospects of securing job offers is paramount. Optimizing this process can lead to more efficient and effective talent acquisition. 
**Competitive Advantage**: In today's competitive job market, organizations are continually vying for the best candidates. The ability to tailor recruitment strategies based on data-driven insights can provide a significant competitive advantage.
**Unique Insights from Agencies**: Data-driven insights can streamline & optimize entire screening, evaluating and matching candidates’ process. So that the agencies can better navigate through latest employment trends and serve diverse industries, offering a broad perspective on placements. 
**Resource Allocation**: Understanding the candidate attributes that matter the most can help the hiring agencies allocate their resources more effectively. It ensures that time and effort are directed towards the aspects that have the greatest impact on securing job offers.
